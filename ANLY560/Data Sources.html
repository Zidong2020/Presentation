pic3.1 		Brief Description about our data:

This part introduces some data sets that need to be used in our project. The data sources of our project mainly include three aspects: the first is the stock price data of real estate companies from jointquant API and Google Finance, the second is the Chinese macroeconomic data from jointquant API, and the third is the public opinion data from jointquant API. The data sources of our project will be introduced and displayed in detail below.

Part One: The historical stock price trend of Companies in the real estate industry

According to the industry classification of China Securities Regulatory Commission (regulator), we sorted out the listed companies in the real estate industry in China's stock market. According to the sorted list of companies, we can obtain the share prices of relevant companies, so as to compare the historical stock price trends of different companies in the later stage. This part can be obtained through joinquant API or Yahoo Finance API. Part Two: Trend of China's macroeconomic situation

This part collects data that can reflect China's macro operation. For example, the statement of total national revenue and expenditure and growth rate (annual) and the statement of basic population situation (considering its impact on the change of house prices from the demand side, such as the change of population structure). At the same time, for the real estate industry, we will collect the table of real estate development investment, the table of real estate development investment by Region (in order to compare the differences of real estate development in different regions), the table of real estate development scale and the growth of construction and completion area by Region (in order to better observe the development of the real estate industry), Housing sales price index of several large and medium-sized cities (monthly). This part can be obtained through joinquant API.
