And not only China, the real estate industry in the United States and the world is constantly growing, and people's living standards have been continuously improved. Below is a more detailed analysis of the portion of the portfolio.
Through data visualization, it can be found that a country's macroeconomic data is closely related to its investment in the real estate industry, and in addition to the country's overall economic development, changes in the country's population will also have a certain degree of impact on real estate. This may be because in addition to being a suitable value-preserving tool at this stage, the fundamental reason for supporting its price is the need for people to live. Therefore, when population inflows decrease, people will become cautious in investing in the real estate industry. And this is directly reflected in people's capital investment in the real estate industry.





In addition, we can find that the investment in real estate in various parts of China is cyclical, because China's investment in the real estate industry in various regions is calculated on an annual basis. In addition, investment in the real estate industry in various parts of China also shows an overall upward trend year by year, which is mainly due to the positive development of China's economic environment and the inflow of population.
At the same time, we also analyzed the stock data of Agree Realty Corporation (ADC), a leading real estate company in the United States. Through analysis, we found that the income of US real estate companies also showed an upward trend year by year (although there were local fluctuations in the data). But at the same time, we can also clearly pay attention to the sudden decline in the company's revenue in 2008 and 2020, which may be the impact of the global financial crisis in 2008 and the epidemic in 2020. It also shows that when a global shock event occurs, all regions are affected. Today, the ties between countries are getting deeper and deeper. In such an environment, there is no so-called high ground that cannot be affected.

But on the whole, there are actually many factors for the development of the real estate industry in a country and regions. In addition to the economic development and population flow in the region, it is also likely to be affected by national regulation and policies. This means that when you are in When making certain investment decisions, our above conclusions can only be used as a more important reference, and you may also need to conduct more detailed analysis and judgment based on actual needs.
