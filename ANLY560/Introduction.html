Topic: The development of China's real estate industry


pic2.1 		Brief introduction:

House is an indispensable consumer product in residents' life. Whether renting or buying, each of us needs a place to live. Therefore, it is very necessary to conduct research on the real estate market. In recent years, the development of China's real estate industry has shown a positive trend, which is inseparable from the development of China's overall economy. On the one hand, with the acceleration of urbanization in China, more and more rural population is moving to cities, which undoubtedly creates a huge demand for housing. On the other hand, the house is also a relatively stable investment target that many people will choose.

However, in recent years, due to the influence of COVID-19 and other factors, the overall development trend of China's real estate industry has changed. What is the reason why house prices have changed from high-speed growth to stable. The reason for this situation may be due to the saturation of demand (population structure problem) on the one hand, and the regulation and control of macro policies on the other hand (Houses are used to live in, not to generate economic benefits - to protect the basic interests of ordinary residents).

Therefore, we believe that in order to achieve better development under the current conditions, real estate enterprises should not only comply with the guidance of national policies, but also actively look for new opportunities and find new opportunities in the existing environment. How to connect traditional industries with modern technology, how to use modern technology to expand and discover people's new needs and create new business opportunities are the key points that should be considered at present.

To sum up, our topic hopes to investigate the development of China's real estate industry from the perspective of time series, in order to grasp the development trend of the real estate industry in the next ten years. For this purpose, we will collect data and conduct comprehensive research from three aspects: the historical stock price trend of companies in the real estate industry, the historical trend of macroeconomic indicators in the same period, and major events in the same period (news and public opinion / macropolicy).
